Jackson Galaxy is a musician who became a cat behaviorist sort of by accident. He is well known from his show on Animal Planet, "My Cat from Hell." In addition to being incredibly vocal about his position against declawing cats, he is also an advocate of TNR (Trap Neuter/spay Return), Spay/Neuter surgeries, and No Kill Nation. Check out the links in this section to learn more about Jackson Galaxy, his foundation, show, work as a cat behaviorist, product lines, and more!

Website

Catster Article

Once featured on Jackson Galaxy's show, "My Cat From Hell," City The Kitty - an orange, polydactyl, Bengal/Manx mix who wandered into his human's life as an incredibly energetic stray with a tendency to cause trouble - has become a spokes-cat of the anti-declaw movement. He has used the fame he gained from being featured on the show to speak out on the truth about declawing, trying to save kitties from suffering. He recently put out a coffee table book of photos of him with proceeds going to help fight to ban declawing
